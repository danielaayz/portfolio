About Me

Hello! I'm a creative problem solver and versatile Front End Developer with a BFA in design. I'm drawn to the endless possibilities of code and passionate about writing clean, functional, and error-free code. My goal is to not only enhance user experiences but also add tangible value to projects through my work.

My passion is on the Front End (HTML, CSS, SaSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, React). I am also interested in back-end development, allowing me to build comprehensive and robust applications.

My background in design means I bring a unique perspective to development, blending aesthetics with functionality. I have a deep understanding of design principles and user-centric approaches, honed through my education and professional experience.

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually outdoors exploring the city or nature, reading, spending time with my partner and our son.
